Welcome to Health is Wealth Restaurant

We are a health-conscious restaurant that serves a variety of nutritious and delicious dishes. Our menu includes options for vegetarians, non-vegetarians, and vegans. We believe that good health is the key to a happy life, and we strive to provide our customers with meals that are both tasty and beneficial for their well-being.

Our Menu

Veg

  • Quinoa Chickpea Salad: Quinoa tossed with chickpeas, cucumbers, tomatoes, and olive oil.
  • Palak Paneer: Cottage cheese cubes cooked in a nutrient-dense, low-fat spinach puree.
  • Moong Dal Cheela: Savory pancakes made from protein-rich, soaked green gram lentils.
  • Baked Oats: Whole oats baked with skimmed milk, berries, flaxseeds, and nuts.
  • Moong Salad: Raw or lightly steamed mung bean sprouts with lemon juice.

Non-Veg

  • Grilled Salmon: Omega-3 rich fish filet grilled with lemon and asparagus.
  • Tandoori Chicken Breast: Lean chicken breast marinated in yogurt and spices, then baked.
  • Egg White Omelet: Protein-packed egg whites whisked with spinach, mushrooms, and onions.
  • Chicken Vegetable Soup: Clear broth simmered with shredded chicken, carrots, and celery.
  • Baked Fish Papillote: Fish cooked inside parchment paper with ginger, garlic, and herbs.

Vegan

  • Tofu Broccoli Stir-Fry: Calcium-rich tofu and broccoli sautéed lightly in sesame oil.
  • Lentil and Vegetable Stew: A thick, fiber-loaded soup of brown lentils, kale, and carrots.
  • Chia Seed Pudding: Chia seeds soaked in almond milk, topped with fresh berries.
  • Avocado Whole-Grain Toast: Mashed avocado on sprouted grain bread with hemp seeds.
  • Brown Rice Buddha Bowl: Edamame, roasted sweet potatoes, and tahini over brown rice.
